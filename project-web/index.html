our showroom

Since 1998, Projects Contemporary Furniture, LC has been a premier showroom in the Midwest for luxury contemporary furniture, lighting, kitchens, closets and bath products in residential and contract settings.

Projects operates as a full service showroom catering to the needs of homeowners, interior designers, builders and architects.

full service design

We have an experienced interior design team, offering a wide range of services including space planning, product & fabric selections, design drawings and much more.

We offer turnkey design services for Poliform cabinetry. Our design team is able to make your vision a reality.
